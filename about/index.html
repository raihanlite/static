About

Welcome to Study Koro. Our goal is to provide helpful educational resources for students in Bangladesh. We publish guides, calculators, and useful information to help students make better academic decisions.

Our website focuses on topics such as:

  • GPA Calculators (SSC, HSC, JSC)
  • University Subject Lists
  • Admission Guides
  • College and University Information
  • Educational Resources for Students

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: make educational information easy to access and understand. Many students struggle to find reliable information online. We aim to solve that by publishing clear and updated guides.

What We Provide

On this website you will find:

  • Accurate academic information
  • Easy-to-use online calculators
  • Lists of universities and subjects
  • Admission related updates

All content is written to be simple, practical, and useful for students.

Why This Website

Many education websites are filled with outdated or confusing information. We try to keep everything clear, updated, and easy to read so students can quickly find what they need.
