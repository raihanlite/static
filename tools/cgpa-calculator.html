How CGPA is Calculated

Formula: CGPA = Σ (Credit × Grade Point) ÷ Σ Credit

Example: Mathematics (3cr × 4.0) + Physics (3cr × 3.75) = 23.25 Total Points. 23.25 ÷ 6 Total Credits = 3.875 CGPA.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is CGPA calculated in Bangladesh universities?

CGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) ÷ Σ(Credit). Bangladesh follows the UGC grading system where A+ = 4.00, A = 3.75, A- = 3.50, and so on.

Which Bangladesh universities are supported?

All major institutions including DU, BUET, RUET, NSU, BRAC, IUB, AIUB, and 40+ more are supported via the UGC preset.

Can I calculate CGPA for multiple semesters at once?

Yes. Use the 'Semester Calculator' for individual terms and the 'Overall Calculator' to combine them into a final cumulative score.

Is this CGPA calculator free and safe to use?

Yes. It is entirely free and uses Browser Local Storage. Your data is never uploaded to any server.
