Bangladesh Education Grading System Overview

Bangladesh uses a 5-point GPA scale for secondary and higher secondary education (SSC and HSC), and a 4-point CGPA scale for university degrees under the University Grants Commission (UGC). Understanding which scale applies to your institution is essential for accurate calculation.

SSC & HSC (5-Point Scale)

The Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) both use the same grading table: A+ = 5.00, A = 4.00, A− = 3.50, B = 3.00, C = 2.00, D = 1.00, F = 0.00. A student's GPA is the average of all subject grade points, with an optional subject bonus if the grade is above C.

University / UGC (4-Point Scale)

All public and private universities accredited by the UGC use a 4.0 CGPA scale: A+ = 4.00, A = 3.75, A− = 3.50, B+ = 3.25, B = 3.00, and so on. CGPA is calculated as the weighted average of grade points multiplied by credit hours.

IELTS (9-Band Scale)

The IELTS exam uses a 9-band scale, where Band 9 is an expert user and Band 1 is a non-user. The overall band is the average of Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking, rounded using the official .25/.75 rule to the nearest whole or half band.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tools free to use?

Yes, all tools on Study Koro are completely free. No account, no subscription, and no hidden charges. They run entirely in your browser, so your data never leaves your device.

Which boards do the GPA calculators support?

The SSC and HSC GPA calculators support all Bangladesh secondary education boards: Dhaka, Rajshahi, Comilla, Chittagong, Sylhet, Barisal, Dinajpur, Mymensingh, and the Madrasah & Technical boards. All use the same official UGC 5-point grading scale.

Which universities does the CGPA calculator support?

The CGPA calculator supports all UGC-affiliated public and private universities including DU, BUET, RUET, KUET, CUET, NSU, BRAC University, IUB, AIUB, DIU, EWU, and 40+ more. University-specific presets are available for institutions with different grading scales such as NSU and BRAC.

How accurate are the IELTS band conversions?

The Listening and Reading raw-to-band conversion tables are based on the official band conversion guidelines published by the British Council and IDP. Note that actual IELTS results are determined by Cambridge Assessment English and minor variations can occur between test forms.

Can I save my results?

The HSC and SSC calculators include a "Save as Image" button that lets you download your result as a PNG file. For the CGPA calculator, all calculations happen in-browser and reset when you close the tab — there is no server-side storage.
