How to Use the SSC GPA Calculator

Step 1 — Select Your Group

Choose Science, Commerce, or Arts from the tabs at the top of the calculator. The subject list updates automatically.

Step 2 — Choose Your Input Method

Select Grades to pick a letter grade (A+, A, A−…) from a dropdown, or Marks to type in your raw score (0–100). You only need to use one — the grade will be set automatically when you enter marks.

Step 3 — Enter Science Electives or Optional Subject

Science students select their 3rd subject (Biology or Higher Math) and 4th subject. Commerce and Arts students can choose an optional 4th subject from their group's list. Leave it blank if not applicable.

Step 4 — Calculate

Click Calculate GPA. Your GPA appears instantly along with your result status — Passed, Failed, or Golden A+ if you scored A+ in every subject.

How SSC GPA is Calculated

The SSC GPA formula used by all Bangladesh education boards works as follows:

  1. Convert each subject's marks to a grade point using the official grading scale.
  2. If any main subject has grade F (0.00), the student is considered failed and GPA = 0.00.
  3. Add all main subject grade points together.
  4. If the optional subject's grade point is above 2.00, add a bonus: (grade point − 2).
  5. Divide the total by the number of main subjects.
  6. Cap the final result at 5.00.

GPA = (Sum of main subject grade points + optional bonus) ÷ number of main subjects

Optional bonus = Grade Point − 2 (only if GP > 2.00, otherwise 0)

Example: 8 subjects with total 36 points + optional A+ bonus (5 − 2 = 3) = 39 ÷ 8 = 4.875 → capped? No → 4.88

What is a Golden A+?

A Golden A+ means scoring A+ (80 marks or above) in every single subject, resulting in a perfect GPA of 5.00. It is the highest possible SSC result in Bangladesh and is widely regarded as a mark of exceptional academic achievement.

SSC Subjects by Group

Science Group

Core subjects: Bangla, English, Mathematics, Religion, ICT, Bangladesh & Global Studies (BGS), Physics, Chemistry. Plus two elective subjects — Biology and/or Higher Math — one of which may be the optional subject.

Commerce Group

Core subjects: Bangla, English, Mathematics, Religion, ICT, General Science. Plus group subjects: Accounting, Business Entrepreneurship, Finance & Banking. One of the group subjects may be taken as the optional subject.

Arts Group

Core subjects: Bangla, English, Mathematics, Religion, ICT, General Science. Plus group subjects: History of Bangladesh & World Civilisation, Geography & Environment, Civics & Citizenship. One may be taken as the optional subject.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is SSC GPA calculated in Bangladesh?

SSC GPA is the average of all main subject grade points. If an optional subject's grade point exceeds 2.00, the excess (GP − 2) is added as a bonus before dividing. An F in any main subject results in a failed result. The maximum GPA is 5.00.

What is a Golden A+ in SSC?

A Golden A+ means scoring A+ (80+ marks) in every subject, giving a perfect GPA of 5.00. It is the highest possible SSC result and is recognized by all Bangladesh education boards.

Does the optional subject increase my GPA?

Yes, but only if your optional subject grade point is above 2.00 (C grade). The bonus is calculated as (grade point − 2). So an A+ in the optional subject adds 3.00 bonus points, while a B (3.00) adds 1.00. The final GPA is still capped at 5.00.

What is the minimum marks to pass SSC?

You need at least 33 marks (Grade D, 1.00 point) in each subject to pass. Scoring 0–32 in any subject results in a grade F (0.00) and the student fails the entire SSC examination regardless of other scores.

Does this calculator work for all Bangladesh boards?

Yes. All eight BISE education boards — Dhaka, Rajshahi, Comilla, Chittagong, Sylhet, Barisal, Dinajpur, and Mymensingh — as well as the Madrasah and Technical boards, use the same official 5-point grading scale for SSC.
