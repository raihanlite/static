HSC GPA Calculator

HSC GPA Calculator is the best and easy way to calculate GPA. You can easily Calculate Grade points without doing any complex math. Using this HSC GPA Calculator anyone can find out their GPA within a few seconds.

HSC Grading System

Marks Points Grades
80 – 100 5.00 A+
70 – 79 4.00 A
60 – 69 3.50 A-
50 – 59 3.00 B
40 – 49 2.00 C
33 – 39 1.00 D
0 – 32 0.00 F

How to Use the GPA Calculator

Step 1: Select Your Group

Choose your academic group from the dropdown menu:

  • Science
  • Business Studies
  • Humanities

The calculator will automatically load the subjects for that group.

Step 2: Enter Marks or Select Grade

For each subject you can enter your result in two ways:

  • Enter Marks in the marks box
  • Or select a Grade from the dropdown

You only need to use one option. If you enter marks, the grade will be calculated automatically.

Step 3: Choose Optional Subject

If you have a 4th / Optional Subject:

  1. Select the subject from the optional subject list
  2. Enter the marks or grade for that subject

If you do not have an optional subject, leave it blank.

Step 4: Calculate GPA

After entering all subjects, click the “Calculate GPA” button.

The calculator will display:

  • Your Final GPA
  • Your Approximate Total Marks

Step 5: Save Your Result

Click “Save as Picture” to download your result as an image.

Important Notes

  • Most subjects are out of 200 marks.
  • ICT is out of 100 marks.
  • Maximum GPA is 5.00.
  • Optional subject points may increase GPA slightly.
