What is the SSC GPA Calculator?

The SSC GPA Calculator is an online tool that helps students calculate their Grade Point Average (GPA) based on their subject grades. Instead of manually adding and dividing grade points, the calculator automatically calculates your final GPA within seconds.

SSC (Secondary School Certificate) is the 10th grade public examination in Bangladesh, and results are measured using the GPA scale of 5.00.

SSC Grading System in Bangladesh

The SSC grading system converts marks into grade points and grades. The highest GPA a student can obtain is 5.00 (A+).

Marks Grade Point Grade
80 – 100 5.00 A+
70 – 79 4.00 A
60 – 69 3.50 A-
50 – 59 3.00 B
40 – 49 2.00 C
33 – 39 1.00 D
0 – 32 0.00 F

This grading system is used for both Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) results in Bangladesh.

How to Calculate SSC GPA

Calculating your SSC GPA is simple if you follow these steps:

Step 1: Select Your SSC Group

First, choose your education group. The main SSC groups are:

  • Science
  • Business Studies (Commerce)
  • Humanities (Arts)

Step 2: Choose Grade Format

Select how you want to calculate your GPA:

  • By Grade (A+, A, A-, etc.)
  • By Grade Point (5.00, 4.00, etc.)

Step 3: Select Your Subjects

Choose the subjects you appeared in from the dropdown list.

Step 4: Select Grade or Points

Enter your grade or grade point for each subject.

Grade values include:

  • A+ = 5.00
  • A = 4.00
  • A- = 3.50
  • B = 3.00
  • C = 2.00
  • D = 1.00
  • F = 0.00

Step 5: Click the Calculate Button

Finally, click the Calculate GPA button. The calculator will automatically show your total GPA result.

SSC New Grading System Update

Due to special circumstances, the SSC examination structure changed in recent years. Instead of the usual 10 subjects, students were evaluated based on 3 main subjects in each group.

Also, the 4th subject system was removed for that exam year.

New SSC Grading System (Marks to Points)

In the updated system, each subject carries 50 marks, and grade points are calculated as follows:

Marks Grade Point Grade
40 – 50 5.00 A+
35 – 39 4.00 A
30 – 34 3.50 A-
25 – 29 3.00 B
20 – 24 2.00 C
17 – 19 1.00 D
0 – 16 0.00 F

SSC GPA Calculation Examples

Science Group Example

Suppose a student obtained:

  • Physics – 44
  • Chemistry – 38
  • Biology – 47

Converted grade points:

  • Physics = 5.00
  • Chemistry = 4.00
  • Biology = 5.00

GPA Calculation:

[ (5.00 + 4.00 + 5.00) ÷ 3 = 4.67 ]

Final GPA = 4.67

Commerce Group Example

Marks obtained:

  • Accounting – 43
  • Finance & Banking – 41
  • Business Entrepreneurship – 48

Grade points:

  • Accounting = 5.00
  • Finance = 5.00
  • Business = 5.00

Calculation:

[ (5 + 5 + 5) ÷ 3 = 5.00 ]

Final GPA = 5.00

Arts Group Example

Marks obtained:

  • History – 38
  • Geography – 25
  • Civics – 32

Grade points:

  • History = 4.00
  • Geography = 3.00
  • Civics = 3.50

Calculation:

[ (4.00 + 3.00 + 3.50) ÷ 3 = 3.50 ]

Final GPA = 3.50

Alternative Method to Calculate GPA

If you are confused with the 50 mark system, there is another simple method.

Just multiply your obtained marks by 2 to convert them into the old 100-mark system.

Example:

  • If you get 45 out of 50, multiply by 2
  • 45 × 2 = 90

Now you can calculate your grade using the old grading system table.

This method produces the same GPA result.
