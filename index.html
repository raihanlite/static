FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from Bangladeshi students.

What tools does Study Koro offer?

Study Koro offers four free tools: an SSC GPA Calculator (Science, Commerce, Arts), an HSC GPA Calculator (Science, Business, Humanities), a University CGPA Calculator (with semester tracking and goal planner), and an IELTS Band Score Calculator (Academic and General Training). All tools are free and require no signup.

Are the GPA calculators accurate for all Bangladesh boards?

Yes. All SSC and HSC calculations use the official grading scale from the Bangladesh Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (BISE), which applies to all boards: Dhaka, Rajshahi, Comilla, Chittagong, Sylhet, Barisal, Dinajpur, Mymensingh, Madrasah, and Technical. The same 5-point scale is used across all boards.

Which universities does the CGPA calculator support?

The CGPA calculator uses the UGC standard 4.0 scale, which applies to all public and private universities accredited by the University Grants Commission of Bangladesh — including DU, BUET, RUET, KUET, CUET, NSU, BRAC University, IUB, AIUB, DIU, EWU, and 40+ more. University-specific grading presets are available for NSU, BRAC, and IUB, which differ slightly from the UGC standard.

What does the optional subject bonus mean in SSC and HSC?

In both SSC and HSC, if you take an optional (4th) subject and score above a C grade (grade point above 2.00), a bonus is added to your total before calculating the GPA. For SSC, the bonus is (grade point − 2). For HSC, the bonus is (grade point − 2) ÷ 2. The final GPA is always capped at 5.00.

What IELTS band do I need for a UK or Australian university?

Requirements vary by institution and program. Most UK undergraduate programs require 6.0–6.5 overall with no band below 5.5. Postgraduate programs typically require 6.5–7.0. Australian universities commonly require 6.5 overall. Always verify with the specific institution, as requirements differ by faculty and degree level.

Is Study Koro free? Is my data safe?

Yes, Study Koro is entirely free to use. All calculations happen locally in your browser — no data is sent to any server, no account is required, and no information is stored or shared. Study Koro also does not display advertising within its calculator tools.
