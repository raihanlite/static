Study Tour Application: Format, Tips, and 5 Examples for Students

Published on March 23, 2026

A study tour is an exciting part of student life. It allows students to visit historical places, industries, museums, universities, and different cities. Through these trips, students learn outside the classroom and gain real world knowledge.

However, before joining a tour, students usually need to submit a study tour application to their teacher, principal, or department head. This application asks for permission to participate in the tour.

In this guide, you will learn what a study tour application is, why schools require it, the proper format, useful writing tips, and several examples you can use as templates.

What Is a Study Tour Application?

A study tour application is a formal letter written by a student requesting permission to join an educational trip organized by a school, college, or university.

The application usually includes:

  • Student information
  • Purpose of the tour
  • Destination
  • Duration of the tour
  • A polite request for permission

Educational tours may include visits to museums, cultural places, industries, or universities. For example, students often visit famous educational locations such as the National Museum of Bangladesh or historical places like the Lalbagh Fort to learn about culture and history.

Because these tours involve travel and responsibility, institutions require formal permission requests.

Why Schools Require a Study Tour Application

Schools and colleges request applications for several important reasons.

1. Official Permission

The application allows teachers or principals to officially approve your participation in the tour.

2. Student Records

Institutions maintain records of students who are attending the trip.

3. Safety and Responsibility

If something unexpected happens during the tour, the school has documentation that the student received official permission.

4. Organization

Applications help schools manage the number of participants and make better arrangements for transportation and accommodation.

Format of a Study Tour Application

Writing a proper application is simple when you follow the correct structure.

1. Receiver Information

Start by addressing the person responsible for approving the tour.

Example:

To The Principal / Head Teacher School or College Name

2. Subject Line

Clearly mention the purpose of the letter.

Example:

Subject: Application for permission to join the study tour

3. Salutation

Use a polite greeting.

Example:

Respected Sir/Madam,

4. Body Paragraph

Explain your request clearly.

Include:

  • Tour destination
  • Tour date
  • Educational purpose

5. Closing

End with a polite request and gratitude.

Example:

I kindly request you to grant me permission to participate in the study tour.

6. Signature

Finish with your details.

Example:

  • Your name
  • Class or department
  • Roll number
  • Date

Example 1: Study Tour Application for School Students

To The Principal ABC High School

Subject: Application for permission to join the study tour

Respected Sir,

I respectfully state that our school is organizing a study tour to the National Museum next week. The tour will help students learn more about the history and culture of our country.

I am very interested in joining this educational trip with my classmates. It will provide valuable learning experience outside the classroom.

Therefore, I kindly request you to grant me permission to participate in the study tour.

Thank you for your consideration.

Yours obediently Raihan Class: 9 Roll: 12 Date: 16 March 2026

Example 2: Study Tour Application for College Students

To The Principal XYZ College

Subject: Application for permission to go on a study tour

Respected Sir,

With due respect, I would like to inform you that our department has arranged a study tour to a historical site next month. The purpose of this tour is to help students gain practical knowledge related to our academic subjects.

I would like to join this tour along with my classmates. I believe the trip will enhance our understanding and provide valuable educational exposure.

Therefore, I kindly request you to allow me to participate in the study tour.

Yours faithfully Raihan Ahmed Department: History Roll: 24 Date: 16 March 2026

Example 3: Short Study Tour Application

To The Head Teacher ABC School

Subject: Permission for study tour

Respected Sir,

Our school is planning a study tour next week. I would like to participate in this educational trip with my classmates.

I kindly request you to grant me permission to join the study tour.

Thank you.

Yours obediently Raihan Class 10 Roll 5

Example 4: Application from Parents

Sometimes schools require permission from parents.

To The Principal ABC High School

Subject: Permission for my son to join the study tour

Respected Sir,

I am the father of Raihan, a student of class nine in your school. I have learned that the school is organizing a study tour next week.

I am happy to allow my son to participate in this educational trip. I believe it will be a great learning opportunity for him.

Thank you for organizing such activities for students.

Sincerely Mr. Karim Parent of Raihan

Example 5: Application for University Study Tour

To The Head of Department Department of Geography XYZ University

Subject: Request for permission to participate in study tour

Respected Sir,

Our department is arranging a study tour as part of the academic program. I am very interested in joining the trip because it will help me understand practical aspects of our course.

I believe the tour will improve our knowledge and provide valuable field experience.

Therefore, I request you to kindly grant me permission to participate in the study tour.

Yours sincerely Raihan Ahmed Department of Geography Student ID: 20345

Tips for Writing a Good Study Tour Application

Follow these tips to write a strong application.

Keep the Language Simple

Use clear and easy sentences so the reader quickly understands your request.

Be Polite

Always maintain a respectful tone when addressing teachers or principals.

Stay Short and Clear

An application should be brief. Avoid unnecessary details.

Include Important Information

Mention:

  • destination
  • purpose
  • date (if known)

Check Grammar

Before submitting, review your application for spelling and grammar mistakes.

Final Thoughts

Study tours are an excellent way for students to learn beyond textbooks. Visiting educational places, museums, and cultural sites helps students gain practical knowledge and new experiences.

Writing a proper study tour application ensures that you receive official permission from your school or college. By following the correct format and maintaining a polite tone, you can easily write an effective application.

You can also use the examples in this guide as templates and adjust them according to your school or college requirements.
